Prateek Yarn

Established in Bhiwandi's textile heartland, Prateek Yarn specialises in fancy and value-added yarn constructions. With over 18,000 spindles and 300+ active product variants, the facility caters to premium shirting, suiting, and home furnishing markets across India and for export.

02
Guntur, Andhra Pradesh

KK Spintex

Located in Guntur's cotton-rich belt, KK Spintex is a high-capacity PV yarn spinning facility. With 12,000+ spindles and a monthly throughput of 350+ MT, the plant specialises in polyester-viscose blends that combine synthetic durability with viscose's natural drape — serving shirting, suiting, and dress material segments.

High-Capacity PV Yarn Manufacturing
KK Spintex
PV Yarn Manufacturing · Guntur, AP
Specialisations
  • 65/35 & 50/50 Polyester–Viscose blends
  • High-volume ring-spinning — 12,000+ spindles
  • Multi-colour space-dyeing & value addition
  • Custom blend ratios on request
  • Shirting, suiting & dress material grade

Manufacturing Process

Process Video — Coming Soon

Guntur Facility
1
Polyester–Viscose Blending

Precise metering of PV fibres in 65/35 or 50/50 ratios — combining durability with natural drape.

2
Draw Frame & Combing

Parallelisation of fibres creates an even, high-lustre sliver ready for ring-frame spinning.

3
Ring Spinning

High-speed ring frames deliver consistent count and twist for downstream weaving and knitting.

4
Auto-Winding & Packing

Automated winding with splice testing ensures zero-defect cones packed to buyer specifications.

03
Palsana, Gujarat

GB Dyed Yarn

Situated near Surat — India's textile export capital — GB Dyed Yarn is the group's colour and finishing hub. Specialising in package dyeing, the facility processes both greige yarn from within the group and third-party input, delivering 500+ shades across a broad range of fancy and standard yarn constructions for home furnishing and apparel.

Premium Dyeing & Texturizing
GB Dyed Yarn
Dyeing & Texturizing · Palsana, GJ
Specialisations

  • HTHP pressure dyeing for deep penetration
  • Fancy & structural yarn constructions
  • Home furnishing & drapery yarn grade
  • RF drying & export-grade rewinding
